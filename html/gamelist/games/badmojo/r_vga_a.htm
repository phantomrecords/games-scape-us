Bad Mojo is a photo-realistic, 3D adventure which places the player in the role of a cockroach, twitching and scrabbling through the cigarette- and rat-carcass littered wasteland of a San Francisco bar/hotel, searching the filth and muck for - well, I'll let the players find that out for themselves.

Pulse Entertainment indicates that orders for Bad Mojo from retailers have thus far exceeded current available product. Although the game has been described by focus

groups as 'Myst meets Blue Velvet,' the appeal should be very broad; the game's navigation is as breezy as an arcade game, and the controls couldn't be more simple - four direction keys and away you go. A suitably ominous soundtrack by Peter Stone rounds out one of the more promising and ghastly cinematic efforts to date.

-Video Game Advisor
