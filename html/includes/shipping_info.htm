In most cases your order will arrive within 48 hours Monday thru Saturday to the exact address you provide provided you place your order before 1pm. Orders placed after 1pm are generally shipped on the following business day. So that means that orders made after 1pm Thursday or Friday count as being placed respectively on Friday or Monday. As a result these orders will arrive after the weekend on Monday or Tuesday. On delivery, our couriers will usually obtain a signature with matching printed name from someone at your address. This practice will vary slightly depending on the way packages are delivered to your area or location. You will already be aware of the standard practice for delivering packages to your are.
